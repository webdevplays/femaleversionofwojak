HOW TO BUY ON UNISWAP

1. Buy Ethereum

Purchase Ethereum to send it to your MetaMask account. You can purchase Ethereum from a centralized exchange such as Binance or Coinbase, purchase directly from MetaMask, or transfer from another wallet.

2. Head to UNISWAP

Contract Address: 0x00000000000000000000000000000000000000

Go to app.uniswap.org. On the upper right of the page click Connect to connect your MetaMask Wallet. You will now see your wallet connected and you can view your Ethereum balance on the middle of the page.

Buy on Uniswap

3. Swap ETH for SPIKE

Click “Select Token” and paste SPIKE address into the search bar:

0x00000000000000000000000000000000000000
‍‍
Select Spike. Now you can type in the amount of Ethereum you wish to swap for SPIKE. When your wallet prompts you, confirm the signatures and you’re done. You’re now officially a SPIKE holder!
