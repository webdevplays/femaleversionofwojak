MATT FURIE'S
FIRST CREATION

Drawn by Matt Furie in kindergarten back in 1984, Spike was the spark of his childhood creativity and the beginning of his artistic adventure.

As the oldest brother in the Boysclub, Spike is always looking after his younger siblings, especially that crazy frog Pepe. Boy, does he have some stories to share about these little gremlins that will leave your jaw on the floor. He tries to keep everybody in check with his friendly, carefree attitude and his knack for bringing people together through shared laughter and adventures.
